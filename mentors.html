Ashima Khetarpal (IT Mentor)

My name is Ashima Khetarpal and I am a Computer Science Educator at Aravali International School, Faridabad. Recently, I was given this incredible opportunity to be a part of an innovative project - Global School Net and Cyber Fair. I was a part of the technical and had the responsibility of ensuring a smooth and error-free Cyber Fair for all the participants. It has been a rewarding experience to collaborate with outstanding educators from around the world.
Working together with future tech-savvy individuals was an invaluable experience. I had the chance to learn how websites are structured and explore various software's. HTML, CSS, Dreamweaver, Javscript, GitHub and Sublime Text were some of the programs I was exposed to and gained valuable knowledge from the same. I was able to expand my skill set and gain a better understanding of web development and design. This project has been an extremely rewarding experience for all involved. As a teacher, I have been able to teach my students valuable skills that will stay with them throughout their lives. My students, in turn, have gained a great deal of knowledge and experience from the project. In addition, the project has enabled us to bridge the divide between the classroom and the real world.
