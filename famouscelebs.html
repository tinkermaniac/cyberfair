Tarla Dalal

Born in Pune, Mrs Tarla Dalal moved to Bombay after her marriage. In 1966, she started cookery classes at her residence. She started writing cookbooks so she could reach out to even more people. She has written a total of 170 titles several of which have been translated into different languages
