Tarla Dalal

Early life and achievements

● Born in Pune, Mrs Tarla Dalal moved to Bombay after her marriage. In 1966, she started cookery classes at her residence.
● She started writing cookbooks so she could reach out to even more people. She has written a total of 170 titles several of which have been translated into different languages like Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi Bengali and even Dutch and Russian.
● In 2005, the Indian merchant chamber honoured Smt. Tarla Dalal is a woman of the year.
● In 2007, Mrs Tarla Dalal was awarded the Padma Shri award.
● Ineeded Mr Lal has left behind a legacy which his son, Sanjay and his team continue to carry forth to ensure that her work benefits the coming generations as well.
