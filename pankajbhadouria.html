Pankaj Bhadouria

Pankaj Bhadouria

Pankaj Bhadouria is the winner of MasterChef India Season 1 in 2010. She was a school teacher who quit a 16-year-old job to participate in the first season of MasterChef India. Cookbook in her name and has written two more cookery books Barbie- I am a Chef, and Chicken from my Kitchen by Bloomsbury publishing.

She has hosted many TV shows those are:

● Chef Pankaj Ka Zayka (Starplus-2011)
● Kifayati Kitchen (Zee Khana Khazana which is nowadays known as Zee Zest 2014-2015)
● 3 Course with Pankaj (Zee Khana Khazana which is nowadays known as Zee Zest-2015)

Social media influence

● She has a Youtube channel named MasterChef Pankaj Bhadouria where she uploads videos on how to make recipes. It has more than 1.34 million subscribers.
● She has a Youtube Shorts channel where she uploads short videos containing tips and a summary of how to make that particular recipe.
● She has her official website-www.pankajbhadouria.com where you can find out about her recipes and life and achievements.
● She is also on Twitter where she posts many things about her achievements and also reposts things.
● She is also on Instagram she has more than 1.2 million followers where she posts recipes in short videos and shares tips she also has a blue tick or she is official.

Her culinary academy

She started her culinary academy in Lucknow on 16 august 2012. It offers Professional Courses and Certificate Courses for amateur chefs.

Her Awards and Recognition

● MasterChef India Winner (Season 1)
● She was featured in the list of 9 people who made it big after participating in reality shows by INDIAN TIMES.
● She is rated as one of the few TV chefs with great integrity for her thoughtful and meticulous recipes on TV shows by INDIAN EXPRESS.
● She Co-hosted the Knorr (brand) Master Class, with MasterChef Australia Judge Chef Gary Mehigan at the World on a Plate Food Festival, in Bangalore, June 2016.
● She was the winner of the Outlook Social Media Award- OSM Kitchen King/Queen of the Year Award by Outlook Magazine, 2016. She was Felicitated by the Honorable President of India, Shri R. N. Kovind as ‘First Ladies’ – an award given by the Ministry of Women & Child Development, Govt. of India.
● She was invited a number of times been invited as a speaker to many prestigious events.

Her Charity Work

● She was Training "Railway Children" in skill development at -Bakery, in association with NGO - Ahsaas.
● She was Cooking 3.0- A Fund Raising Cookery Workshop for Make A Difference at Radisson Blu, New Delhi
● She Created Lucknow's Largest Community Kitchen for Under Privileged Children on 14 August 2016 at the Pankaj Bhadouria Culinary Academy, Lucknow. The event was created in association with the Child's Right Commission, Govt. of Uttar Pradesh.
